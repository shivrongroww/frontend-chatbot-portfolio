# About Me

> Write 2–3 sentences as if you're introducing yourself at the start of an interview.
> This is what the chatbot will use when someone asks "tell me about yourself."

Example:
"I'm a product designer with 5 years of experience working on B2B SaaS products.
I care most about making complex workflows feel simple, and I work best at the
intersection of user research and interaction design. I'm currently based in [city]
and open to [remote / hybrid / on-site] roles."

---

## Background

Where did you start and how did you get here? 2–4 paragraphs covering:
- Your origin (how you got into design)
- What you've worked on broadly
- What has shaped how you think about design
- Where you are now and what you're looking for

## Working Style and Values

How do you work best? What do you care about? This helps the chatbot answer
culture-fit questions authentically.

- How you collaborate with PMs, engineers, and stakeholders
- How you handle ambiguity or disagreement
- What kind of feedback culture you thrive in
- Design values you hold strongly (e.g. simplicity, accessibility, craft, speed)
